A DevOps class is an essential educational opportunity for aspiring professionals in the world of software development and IT operations.

DevOps classes typically cover a wide range of topics, including version control, continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD),
containerization.

In COMP 3104 , we will gain hands-on experience with popular DevOps tools like Git, Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes, enabling them to streamline
development processes, enhance collaboration between development and operations teams, and deploy software more reliably and efficiently
