George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and is named after George Brown, who was one of the Fathers of Confederation in Canada. 
The college offers a wide range of diploma, certificate, and degree programs across a variety of fields including business, community services, health sciences, hospitality, and culinary arts, among others.

George Brown College is known for its strong emphasis on applied learning and has a number of partnerships with industry and community organizations. The college operates through three main campuses in downtown Toronto - St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and Waterfront Campus.
